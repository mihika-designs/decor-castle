Our Story

Two decades of trust.
One bold new vision.

Padmawati Enterprises was born from a single loan, a small room, and an enormous belief in hard work. What began in Indore as a modest adhesive distribution business grew — through reinvention, dedication, and an ever-expanding dealer network — into the most comprehensive interior surfaces enterprise in Madhya Pradesh.

Today, we carry the widest range of decorative sheets in the state, representing India's finest surface brands. And now, we are building an Experience Center — seven floors of curated material discovery — where every surface tells its story and every finish finds its home.
